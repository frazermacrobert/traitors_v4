How to Play

Each day, you’ll face a workplace scenario.
1️⃣ Pick the safest choice — avoid risky behaviour.
2️⃣ Unsafe behaviour means instant elimination.
3️⃣ Stay safe to see what everyone else did.
4️⃣ Vote off the player you suspect of sabotage.
5️⃣ Keep going until all traitors are gone (win) or they take over (lose).
If votes tie, the group decides automatically.
